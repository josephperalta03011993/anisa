Obituary

Anisa Dagohoy Peralta a beacon of charity, love, kindness, and generosity, passed away peacefully at her home on December 10, 2024, surrounded by her loved ones. Born on September 10, 1964 in Aklan. Anisa devoted her entire life in the service to others. She is a beloved mother, wife, and friend who found joy in the simplest aspects of life.

Anisa was known for her dedication in serving in the church of Jesus Christ of Latter-day Saints. Her relentless dedication to her family and others. Her spirit lives on through her two loving children, husband, friends, and innumerable lives she touched throughout her life.
